重点项目

Stellaris AI
Stellaris AI是一家专注于生成式人工智能技术研发和应用的香港大学衍生公司，于2023年5月10日在香港大学发布了香港地区自主研发的首个千亿参数级别通用大语言模型 - SGPT。当前，SGPT系列大语言模型的最新产品SGPT-3.0在多项主流评测指标已达到国际领先水平。其采用的内生安全技术，实现了基于模型层的生成内容安全性保障，可以为各类应用提供了更加安全和稳定的底层技术支撑。
Minecraft AI 开源社区
旨在基于Minecraft虚拟环境构建面向具身智能图灵测试的实验平台，重点探索AIC（AI Character）记忆系统、行动规划机制等具身智能核心技术。同时合作开展教学、科普、研究与技术交流，与开源中国等伙伴共同推动涵盖大模型架构、训练、优化和智能体构建的生成式人工智能技术和应用普及，与开发者和科研工作者共同创建先进、开放的前沿技术创新和共享生态。

Key Projects

Stellaris AI
Stellaris AI is an HKU spin-off company focused on generative AI research and applications. On May 10, 2023, it launched SGPT, Hong Kong's first self-developed general-purpose large language model with hundreds of billions of parameters. The latest SGPT-3.0 has achieved international leading levels in multiple mainstream benchmarks. Its intrinsic safety technology provides content security at the model level, offering safer and more stable technical support for various applications.
Minecraft AI Community
Aims to build an experimental platform for embodied intelligence Turing tests based on Minecraft virtual environment, focusing on core technologies such as AIC (AI Character) memory systems and action planning mechanisms. Collaborates on education, outreach, research and technical exchange, partnering with Open Source China and others to promote generative AI technologies covering large model architecture, training, optimization and agent construction, creating an advanced, open ecosystem for frontier technology innovation and sharing with developers and researchers.
